Wearable DMM for embedded use with a TFT touch-screen color display. Analog features such as V, I, R and digital functions such as Fin & out, DC, PWM in & out, LA. Form factor allows for wrist mounting, desktop mounting, or fold up to fit in a pocket. Rechargeable.

Features

  • Voltage: 0-30V DC
  • Current: 0-1A DC
  • Resistance: 0-1Mohm
  • Frequency counter: 0-10Mhz
  • Pulse generator: 0-100Mhz
  • PWM in
  • PWM out
  • Logic Analyzer: TBD
  • TFT Touch-screen color display with adjustable brightness
  • Large 64-point primary font
  • Probes: 2mm probes, or 4-pin connector
  • USB-rechargeable LiPo battery
